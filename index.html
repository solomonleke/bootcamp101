About me

“There are three responses to a piece of design – yes, no, and WOW! Wow is the one to aim for."
Milton Glaser
I'm a web designer who lives by this code of perfection and simplicity, I strive to make sure that all my pieces of design get the response "WOW". More so, the life of a designer is a life of fight. Fight against the ugliness. Just like a doctor fights against disease. For me, the visual disease is what we have around, and what I try to do is cure it somehow with designs I produce.
I love challenging my creativity by picking up complex designs and simplify them because I believe “Always Learning, Always Growing”

When I'm not designing, you can find me looking up designs online, listening to music or playing video games.

03

How
I Work

Understand 01

Understand the requirements, create user personas, define use cases. Having a knowledge about the user and his environment helps to provide a clear direction for the design.

Research 02

Analyze competitors, research the latest trends while keeping an eye on the guidelines. This helps define the target, see similar projects and validate an idea before developing any solution.

Sketch 03

After gathering ideas on all aspects of job, I sketch, wireframe and design evaluate and make corrections.


Design 04

Design the images and create prototypes for the job owner or user to have a view of what has been done.

Iteration 05

Do a review of the design with project owner. Sometimes projects demands adjustments and rethinking based on users criteria. I apply improvements on the project before hosting.

Host 06

Once the project is approved after we are ready to host, i get a domain and publish the web site.
